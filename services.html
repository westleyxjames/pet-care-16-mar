Our Pet Care Services

Comprehensive care solutions tailored to meet your pet's unique needs

Dog Walking

Our professional dog walking service ensures your canine companion gets the exercise and outdoor time they need. Whether your dog needs a quick potty break or an extended adventure, our experienced walkers provide safe, engaging walks tailored to your dog's energy level and needs.

  • Maintains your dog's physical health and fitness
  • Provides mental stimulation and reduces boredom
  • GPS tracking and real-time updates

Starting at $20 per walk

Pet Sitting

When you're away from home, our pet sitting service provides your furry friends with the care and attention they deserve in the comfort of their own environment. Our sitters ensure your pets maintain their regular routines and receive plenty of love and companionship.

  • Reduces pet stress by staying in familiar surroundings
  • Daily photo and video updates
  • Plant watering and mail collection included

Starting at $35 per visit

Pet Grooming

Our professional pet grooming service keeps your pets looking and feeling their best. From basic baths to full grooming packages, our experienced groomers provide gentle, thorough care using high-quality, pet-safe products.

  • Maintains healthy skin and coat
  • Nail trimming and ear cleaning
  • Breed-specific styling available

Starting at $45 per session

Pet Boarding

When you need extended care for your pets, our boarding facility provides a home-away-from-home experience. Our spacious, clean accommodations and attentive staff ensure your pets are comfortable, safe, and happy during their stay.

  • Clean, climate-controlled facilities
  • 24/7 professional supervision
  • Daily updates and photos for owners

Starting at $50 per night

Pet Wellness Check

Our pet wellness check service provides basic health monitoring to help you keep track of your pet's well-being. While not a substitute for veterinary care, our trained staff can identify potential concerns and provide peace of mind about your pet's daily health.

  • Regular weight and vital monitoring
  • Detailed wellness reports
  • Recommendations for veterinary follow-up if needed

Starting at $30 per check

Puppy & Kitten Care

Young pets require special attention and care. Our puppy and kitten care service is designed specifically for the unique needs of growing pets, providing the gentle handling, socialization, and supervision they need during their crucial early months.

  • Age-appropriate play and socialization
  • Potty training support and reinforcement
  • Safety-focused environment

Starting at $40 per session
