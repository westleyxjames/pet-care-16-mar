Cookie Policy

Last Updated: March 12, 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners. Cookies help us understand how visitors use our website and enable us to improve your browsing experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

PetComfort Hub uses cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Understand how you use our website
  • Improve our website's functionality and user experience
  • Analyze website traffic and user behavior
  • Provide personalized content and recommendations
  • Remember your cookie consent preferences

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 Essential Cookies

Purpose: These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and accessibility.

Duration: Session cookies (deleted when you close your browser) or up to 1 year

Can be disabled: No - These are essential for the website to work

3.2 Functional Cookies

Purpose: These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization, such as remembering your preferences, language settings, and previous form inputs.

Duration: Up to 1 year

Can be disabled: Yes - But some features may not work properly

3.3 Analytics Cookies

Purpose: These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. This helps us improve our website and services.

Duration: Up to 2 years

Can be disabled: Yes

3.4 Marketing Cookies

Purpose: These cookies track your online activity to help us deliver more relevant advertising or to limit how many times you see an advertisement.

Duration: Up to 2 years

Can be disabled: Yes

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may also use third-party cookies from trusted partners to help us:

  • Google Analytics: To analyze website traffic and user behavior
  • Social Media Platforms: To enable social media sharing and integration
  • Payment Processors: To process payments securely
  • CDN Providers: To deliver content quickly and reliably

These third parties have their own privacy policies and cookie policies. We recommend reviewing their policies to understand how they use cookies.

6. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. You can manage your cookie preferences in several ways:

6.1 Cookie Banner

When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie banner where you can accept or reject non-essential cookies.

6.2 Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. Here's how to manage cookies in popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies and website data
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings > Privacy, search, and services > Cookies and site permissions

6.3 Opt-Out Links

For third-party cookies, you can opt out directly:

  • Google Analytics: Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Network Advertising Initiative: NAI Opt-out

7. Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you choose to disable cookies, some features of our website may not function properly:

  • You may need to manually enter information each time you visit
  • Some personalized features may not be available
  • You may see less relevant content and advertisements
  • Certain interactive features may not work correctly

8. Do Not Track Signals

Some browsers include a "Do Not Track" (DNT) feature that signals to websites that you do not want to have your online activity tracked. Currently, there is no industry standard for responding to DNT signals. At this time, our website does not respond to DNT signals.

9. Updates to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legislation, or our business practices. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting a notice on our website or updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

10. More Information
